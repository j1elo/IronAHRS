Yaw, Pitch, Roll
================

Definición:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flight_dynamics_(fixed_wing_aircraft)
In flight dynamics, pitch, roll and yaw angles measure both the absolute attitude angles and changes in attitude angles. These are defined as:
* Pitch (Y axis): angle of X body axis (nose) relative to horizon. Also a positive (nose up) rotation about Y body axis.
* Roll (X axis): angle of Y body axis (wing) relative to horizon. Also a positive (right wing down) rotation about X body axis.
* Yaw (Z axis): angle of X body axis (nose) relative to North. Also a positive (nose right) rotation about Z body axis.
